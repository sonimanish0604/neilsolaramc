Problem

Weak field evidence slows execution, approvals, and payments.

What still happens in the field

  • Paper checklists and handwritten readings
  • Photos stored on technician phones
  • Manual report preparation after visits
  • WhatsApp-based follow-up and fragmented evidence

What this breaks for the business

  • Customer-side approvals take longer
  • AMC vendor payments get delayed while paperwork is reviewed
  • Historical retrieval across sites becomes painful
  • Analytics and reporting remain incomplete or unreliable

Product System

A closed-loop workflow from field visit to customer-approved record.

  1. Supervisor creates and assigns a work order.
  2. Technician executes checklist tasks and captures field evidence.
  3. Photos, signatures, and inverter readings are attached to the workflow.
  4. Backend generates structured reports and manages approval state.
  5. Customer-side review and sign-off complete the proof loop.
  6. Records remain available for retrieval, reporting, and future analysis.

AI-Assisted Evidence

AI is being applied where it reduces friction and improves trust.

Active evidence roadmap

  • OCR-assisted inverter serial extraction during asset registration
  • OCR-assisted inverter reading extraction during service visits
  • Confidence scoring and review-required status handling
  • Geo-validation of field evidence relative to the site
  • Offline-capable mobile evidence workflow refinement

Practical AI, not decorative AI

The product uses AI as assistive infrastructure for operational evidence capture. On-device ML assistance and backend review paths are meant to make field work faster, cleaner, and more reliable, while preserving human review when confidence is low.

Architecture

Offline-capable mobile execution with a disciplined system of record.

Execution path

Assigned work orders Mobile field capture Offline draft and sync layer Backend APIs and validation Postgres + Cloud Storage Signed reports and historical retrieval

System disciplines

  • Mobile workflow continues in weak-network environments
  • On-device assistance improves OCR-oriented capture UX
  • Cloud Storage handles evidence files and report assets
  • Postgres remains the final source of truth
  • Backend APIs validate, persist, and report on accepted records
  • Cloud Run and Cloud Build support deployment discipline
