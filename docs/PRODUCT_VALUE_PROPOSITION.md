# NEIL Solar AMC – Digital Proof & Compliance System

## Overview

NEIL Solar AMC is a digital inspection and proof system designed for Solar EPC companies managing Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC).

It replaces paper-based inspection processes with structured digital reporting, photo evidence, and dual signatures, delivered instantly to customers.

The goal is simple:
Reduce disputes. Increase transparency. Save time.

---

## Problem We Solve

Traditional AMC processes rely on:

- Paper checklists
- Handwritten meter readings
- Photos stored in technician phones
- Delayed or inconsistent reporting
- Manual signature collection
- Difficulty retrieving past records

This leads to:

- Disputes over plant performance
- Difficulty proving site visit completion
- Missing historical data
- Technician turnover risk
- Time-consuming document retrieval
- Poor customer confidence

---

## Our Solution

NEIL Solar AMC provides:

- Structured digital checklist
- Mandatory net meter readings
- Inverter data capture
- Before/after panel cleaning photos
- Time-stamped image evidence
- Technician digital signature
- Customer supervisor digital signature
- Instant report generation
- WhatsApp-based report sharing
- Easy monthly and site-based retrieval

---

## Key Benefits for EPC Companies

### 1. Dispute Protection

Every visit includes:

- Net meter photo proof
- Inverter readings
- Site condition images
- Dual digital signatures
- Time-stamped records

If a dispute arises months later, the proof is immediately available.

### 2. Reduced Technician Dependency

Technicians come and go.
